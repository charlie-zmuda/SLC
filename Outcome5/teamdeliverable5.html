Welcome!

Explore Minneapolis Parks is the best go-to website for people wondering what the parks around Minneapolis have to offer. It is our hope that in the future when you are thinking of what park you would like to visit on any given day, you would go to this website first! Here we show some of the great parks Minneapolis has to offer. Whether you're looking for a park to bring your dog, or if you're thinking about walking around and seeing the art. Be sure to explore all the pages above!
