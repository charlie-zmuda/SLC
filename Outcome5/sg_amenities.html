Sculpture Garden Amenities

The Minneapolis Sculpture Garden is an 11-acre park located near and operated by the Walker Art Center in affiliation with the Minneapolis Park and Recreation board. With over 40 permanent art installations and temporary pieces that come and go periodically. The Sculpture Garden is also within walking distance to Loring Park and the Basillica of Saint Mary.


  • Address: 725 Vineland Pl, Minneapolis MN
  • Phone: (612) 375-7600
  • Hours: Monday-Sunday: 6AM-10PM

  • Click Here For Directions

Parking can be found underground at the Walker Center on-site. The daily rate is $4 for up to 12 hours which is subject to change on special events. Ramp can be entered on Vineland Place at Bryant Avenue. Additional paid parking is available in the Minneapolis Sculpture Garden lot. Free parking is available on the surrounding streets though limited.
